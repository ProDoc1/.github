**Building trust in healthcare decisions through transparency and verification.**

ProDoc is a centralized digital platform designed to provide patients in Sri Lanka with structured, transparent, and verified data about healthcare professionals. By aggregating qualifications, surgical history, and evidence-backed reviews, ProDoc eliminates the uncertainty of finding the right doctor.
